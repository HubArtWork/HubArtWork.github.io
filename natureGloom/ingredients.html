Why coal?

The answer is simple, coal is porous and attracts impurities, bacteria, and oil like a magnet. On top of all that, coal is not drying. Known for a long time for its virtues, all the positive aspects of activated vegetable carbon have been rediscovered for some time now.

It clarifies the complexion and softens the skin. According to several doctors, one of its main assets is its granular texture that allows to practice a natural exfoliation without going through the plastic beads box contained in formulas straight out of cosmetic laboratories. It ensures a gentle mechanical exfoliation without the risk of breaking the epidermis.
