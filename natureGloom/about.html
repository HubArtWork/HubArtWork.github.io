Our goal is to offer a high quality product while remaining natural and ecological. NatureGloom aims at being a leader and a reference in terms of the results obtained from the application of our products on your skin.

Team & values

Our team is made up of passionate people in the field such as cosmeticians, laboratory specialists, field representatives, salespeople and a complete group of employees working at our warehouse. NatureGloom also has several independent external consultants as we perform thousands of quality, safety and performance tests on our products.

Under the management of Mr. Campeau, our team works hard to offer the best products and the best service to ensure customer satisfaction. Our human resources management policy promotes a strong mix of our teams. We are committed to respecting the legal and regulatory conditions relating to our products.

Transparency is the value that guides our daily work. We have a vision that is inspired by the well-being and health of the skin. We listen to each other to ensure the effectiveness of our products and our laboratory has high standards of quality and safety. Our production chain is observed and analyzed from beginning to end in excellent manufacturing conditions.
