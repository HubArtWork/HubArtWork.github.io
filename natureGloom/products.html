Face moisturizer

Our anti-aging face lotion contains the best ingredients known to date for the maintenance of healthy, supple and moisturized facial skin. NatureGloom provides a fresh feeling from the first application and is easy to apply.

Moisturizing the skin is indeed one of the ways to slow down epidermal aging since we maintain or increase the amount of water it contains. NatureGloom is made with activated carbon, several vitamins including vitamin C and vitamin P, artichoke leaf extracts and several ingredients that work on your skin to improve and protect it every day. This lotion awakens your skin’s radiance and the uniformity of your complexion by nourishing it intensely.
