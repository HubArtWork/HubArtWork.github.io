You want do buy our skin care but already have the box packaging?

We offer you to replace your product without having to buy the box packaging again! Send us back your NatureGloom luxury box (without fees) and we will send you back our skin care lotion jar to a reduce price!

How does it work?

1

Upon reception of the package, you will receive a return label/tag. Keep it.

2

The next time you order our product, choose the ‘’Refill’’. We will send you a NatureGloom shipping box. You must place your wooden box inside following the instructions, close the box and stick the label/tag on it.

3

Once we get your box by express shipping, we will send you back your product within 2 business days.

Note that the jar will be brand new, but the wooden box will be the exact same that you send us.
