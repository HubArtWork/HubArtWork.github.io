About us

We all have different goals, but at the end of the day, it’s all about being able to maintain this lifestyle for as long as possible. At Shape Me, we commit to offering a bespoke nutritional experience to all customers. Providing a tailored service that fits everyone's specific goals and needs - including the everyday food enthusiast.

We are revolutionizing the meal prep industry by bringing forth solutions to current outdated models. We offer services catered to every clients needs. Our team is comprised of personal trainers who have a deep love for food, and we ensure your goals are met without sacrificing taste and the variety of meals you can have! So whether you’d like to lose weight, gain weight, maintain, or just want to eat amazing healthy food, we’ve got you covered.

As an added service, please feel free to contact us to set up a consult with our trainers to help you reach your goals, with ShapeMe, we take care of the hard part, all you have to do is eat!
