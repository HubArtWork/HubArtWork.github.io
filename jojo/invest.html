What is an index portfolio?

Cryptocurrency index funds allow you to get access to the entire market at no extra cost (to investments in digital assets). For example, the top 20 index will itself track the best coins by captilization in the entire market: buy them and sell those that are off the list.

Thus, the portfolio is constantly automatically revised and rebalanced - changing the cell of the fund, taking into account price movements.The indices do not require understanding the market and spending time on analyzing and rebuilding the portfolio, while they have better static returns than more than 90 percent of active investors

Why Invest in an Index?

Index funds have a long history of proven performance and have consistently outperformed actively managed funds in traditional markets over the past 30 years.

Fact 95% of traders cannot beat the market index. It works the same way and for cryptocurrencies, the index hike with cryptocurrencies has already shown the best balanced return. in comparison with the bitcoin Eth and other cryptocurrencies, and over the past 5 years, it has also significantly surpassed gold, the SNP500 index and other traditional financial instruments
