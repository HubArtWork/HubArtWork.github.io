Startup Pitch Contests

First, log into your account with login and password info you have received via email. After that, choose your evaluation category.

Then you will see the list of startups that were approved to take part in the contest. Learn more about them by clicking on the "Info" button. After you have viewed the info page, you will be able to evaluate each startup.

Lastly, do not forget to rate the startups: you can do so by clicking on the "Rate" button.

Keep in mind that you will receive an email reminder in case you have not evaluated any of the startups.

Before the final pitch presentation, the startup list will be updated. You will be able to evaluate every pitch during the Pitch presentation session on the 4th of September.

Your points for presentation will be automatically added to the points that you have assigned during the first evaluation stage before the finals.

In case of any questions, do not hesitate to contact Pitch Contest Support at pitch@inclusionforum.global.
