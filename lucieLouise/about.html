About

Lucy & Louis’ story began in August 2020. Powered by TripleOne and inspired by some family members that were already working in the field, Lucy & Louis’ hair salon and amusement center for kids by subscription was born.
Become a Member

Our idea

We had the idea to bring something that every family of our modern time will need and love: beauty, freedom, amusement, and hobbies at the same place for our kids. All of this at one fix price per month.

There is a passion for all types of experiences. We cut newborn’s hair no matter how old they are, celebrate birthday parties, organization of thematic evening, rental of our game room for your private parties for children (on demand) etc...The knowledge that we have is priceless and the concept is unique!

Take note that every membership of 3 months or more, you will get a free access to our different events and thematic evenings plus 20% off on our birthday party package to celebrate your kid's birthday in our amusement center.
