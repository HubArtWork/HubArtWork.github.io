Rules

To ensure everyone’s safety and full enjoyment of the Facility the following rules must be followed on premises:

Socks only sections

The playroom section of Lucy&Louis is a SOCKS ONLY sections. No shoes, slippers or bare feet are allowed. Socks are available for purchase at the Facility at 3$ per pair.

Facility Waiver

The Facility Waiver MUST be filled out PRIOR to entering the playroom section. The waiver canbe filled out online or in person.

Specific age groups

The playroom areas are restricted to specific age groups as stated at the Facility. Children under 3 years old are NOT ALLOWED to enter these areas.

Candies

Popcorn, individually rapped candies or lollipops are NOT ALLOWED.

Outside meals

Outside meals are NOT ALLOWED.

Exit center

Children may only exit the center with their responsible parent, caregiver or guardian.

Food, gum or drink

Food, gum or drink is NOT ALLOWED in the play areas. Food and drink may be consumed in designated areas around the facilities only.

No party common areas

No party celebrations are allowed in the common or hair salon areas i.e. cake cutting, gift opening, etc.

Catered through Lucy&Louis

All food for birthday parties and events must be catered through Lucy&Louis.

Food & Cake orders

All food&cake orders or changes must be made at least 5 days before your party or event. Lucy&Louis will make every effort to accommodate your requests or changes, but orders or changes made less than 3 days before your party cannot be guaranteed.

lost or stolen items

Lucy&Louis is not responsible for any lost or stolen items. For the safety of our staff and customers, the facility is monitored via video surveillance.

Free of illness

Please ensure that you and children and accompanying adults are free of illness. Lucy&Louis reserves the right to refuse entry or ask people to leave if there are obvious signs of contagious illness.

No tape or any form

No tape or any form of adhesives on any of the Facility walls.
