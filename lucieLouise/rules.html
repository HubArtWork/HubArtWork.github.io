Rules

To ensure everyone’s safety and full enjoyment of the amusement center, the following rules must be followed at all times on site:

Covid-19 disclaimer: Dear clients, following the COVID-19 pandemic, our team has put in place extra sanitary mesures to insure your safety as well as our employee’s. We kindly require all clients to respect the rules presented at the entrance of our establishment. Thank you for your understanding, we are looking forward to receiving you !

Candies

Popcorn, individually rapped candies or lollipops are NOT ALLOWED.

Outside meals

Outside meals are NOT ALLOWED.

Exit center

Children may only exit the center with their responsible parent, caregiver or guardian.

Food, gum or drink

Food, candy, gum or drinks are NOT ALLOWED in the playground areas. Food and drinks may be consumed in designated areas around the facilities only.

No party common areas

No party celebrations are allowed in the common or hair salon areas i.e. cake cutting, gift opening, etc.

Catered through Lucy & Louis

All food for birthday parties and events must be catered through Lucy & Louis.

Food & Cake orders

All food and cake orders or changes must be made at least 5 days before your party or event. Lucy & Louis will make every effort to accommodate your requests but orders or changes made less than 3 days before your party cannot be guaranteed.

lost or stolen items

Lucy & Louis is not responsible for any lost or stolen items. For the safety of our staff and customers, the facility is monitored via video surveillance.

Free of illness

Please ensure that you and children and accompanying adults are free of illness. Lucy & Louis reserves the right to refuse entry or ask people to leave if there are obvious signs of contagious illness.

No tape or any form

No tape or any form of adhesives on any of the Facility walls.
