MIDHUB

Home for your Uniqueness

Decentrilized platform for a conscious person who wants to interact safely and effectively with the world without losing control over their data, as well as for people-oriented organizations.

What drives us
  • Idea
  • Human
  • Technology

Each person is a unique individual who is destined to prove himself in the world around him based on his rights and freedom of choice. One of the manifestations of its uniqueness is the personal data, on the basis of which he interacts with society.

Unfortunately, nowadays, many of us have certainly lost control of our data, resulting in the loss of some of our integrity and rights. Our main goal is to create a platform where people of all ages and nationalities can have security and control over their private data. We believe that the result will be an environment in which people can regain some of their integrity and take control of their data and take a confident step into expressing their uniqueness.

New relationship

MIDHUB platform is a new, more advanced way of personal data management and protection for the individuals and the organizations with whom they cooperate. Thanks to technological advances, including the increasing complexity of smartphones, decentralized storage, cryptography, blockchain and smart contract technology, creation of a more secure and efficient way to manage data for the user and those who process this data became possible.

For web-services
  • Efficiency
  • Security
  • GDPR

For web resources MIDHUB is a new way to offer your customers one-click registration and identity verification, as well as dramatically improve the efficiency of your business processes and the level of security processing of personal data in accordance with GDPR requirements.

What MIDHUB offers for an individual?
  • Management of personal identification documents through your mobile phone and, if lost, instant access to them through the internet from anywhere in the world
  • Registration and proof of identity on web resources in a single click.
  • User-friendly dashboard for processing and storage of customer's personal data
  • Complete control and management of data transferred to web resources and other organizations at the time of registration or identity verification.
  • A single password to access the entire Internet space.
  • A convenient support channel and dedicated information feed with the organizations you have provided data to.
  • Secure, decentralised storage of your data to which no one has access but you, which is managed by a smart contract.
