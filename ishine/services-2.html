  • When should you use deep cleaning?
  • In short, deep cleaning is used when common cleaning (spring cleaning, regular cleaning, etc.) does not give the required results in terms of quality.

Deep cleaning is also necessary to remove dust and dirt from hard-to-reach places. Another typical use of deep cleaning is the removal of complex contaminants.
