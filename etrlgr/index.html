The Reliable Email Service

We know your messages are highly sensitive. Let us take good care of them.

What is Etlgr?

Big-name mail engines use shared IP addresses for all their clients. If one of those gets flagged as a spammer, the entire server is banned and all of the mail bounces. When your emails carry sensitive data, you can’t risk it. That’s where Etlgr comes in.

Etlgr is a cloud mail service (CMS) that allows you to send and receive mail from within any application. Powered by private IP deployment, Etlgr ensures exceptional deliverability and protects sender reputation. Your emails are in good hands.

Where is Etlgr needed most?

The applications are virtually limitless. Here are just a few:
Transactional emails

Send all sorts of trigger-based communication: balance statements, order confirmations, shipment updates and so much more.

CRM Notifications

Don’t let any important business updates go unnoticed. Set up staff e-mail notifications by integrating Etlgr into your software infrastructure via API.

Logistics

Send out timely e-mail notifications on your deliveries and shipments. With Etlgr it’s much easier than you might think.
